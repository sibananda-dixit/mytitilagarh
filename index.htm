About Titilagarh

Exploring Titilagarh:

It is one of the subdivision of Bolangir District.It is also 2nd biggest town in bolangir.It is One of the Hottest place in India

Importance of Titlagarh

Railway Station

It is the popular Railway Junction in western Odisha.It is also most profit making junction both in goods and passengers.It is connected to all important cities of India.

People from Kalahandi,Balangir and Sonepur depend on this station.

This station has 3 platforms.Near about 100 trains are passing through this station.

Lord Gupteswar Temple

Titilagarh is famous for Lord Gupteswar temple which is situated in the KUMUDA hill.Every year Shivaratri Festival is celebrated in this temple where lakhs of pilgrims visit the place during this festival.
